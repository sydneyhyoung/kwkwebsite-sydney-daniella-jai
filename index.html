The Basics

At the WeWork building in Burbank, CA the Kode with Klossy instructors work closely with young, talented girls to develop coding skills throughout a two week process.

  • Students are provided with laptops to use for coding practice.
  • Lunch is served at 12:00 p.m. to fuel the creative processes.
  • Time is alotted for improv/games to clear the mind and develop relationships between the girls.
